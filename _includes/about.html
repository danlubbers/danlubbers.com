About

Dan Lubbers is a Photographer/High End Retoucher primarily based in Louisville, Kentucky. A love of the outdoors has led Lubbers to places around the world like performing service work in Guatemala and roaming the pyraminds of Ancient Egypt. Lubbers got his start with Extreme sports photography when he went on the La Sportiva Solutions Climbing Tour in 2007. Lubbers has really delved into a passion for environmental portraiture. A love of good coffee usually leads one to find him at local coffee shops working diligently on his work.
