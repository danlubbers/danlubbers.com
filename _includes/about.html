About

Dan Lubbers is a Photographer primarily based in Louisville, Kentucky. A love of the outdoors has led Lubbers to places around the world like performing service work in Guatemala and roaming the pyraminds of Ancient Egypt. Lubbers got his start with Extreme sports photography and has really delved into a passion for environmental portraiture. A love of good coffee usually leads one to find him at local coffee shops working diligently on his work.
